# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** March 19, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 19, 2026

## Overview

ARIA (Ambient Reasoning & Intelligence Assistant) is a personal, self-hosted voice and messaging assistant built and operated by an individual for private, non-commercial use. This privacy policy describes how information is handled when you interact with ARIA via SMS, MMS, or other channels.

## Data We Collect

When you communicate with ARIA, the following data may be collected:

- Phone number of the sender
- SMS and MMS message content, including text and media (images, files)
- Date and time of messages
- Calendar events, reminders, and personal notes voluntarily provided

## How Your Data Is Used

All data collected is used solely to provide personal assistant functionality, including:

- Responding to questions and commands
- Managing calendar events and reminders
- Analyzing images or files sent to the service
- Tracking health, nutrition, and personal log entries at the user's request

## Data Storage and Security

- All data is stored locally on privately owned hardware secured behind an encrypted private network (Tailscale mesh VPN).
- No data is stored on third-party cloud platforms or shared databases.
- Access to stored data is restricted to the service operator only.

## Data Sharing

Your information is **not sold, rented, traded, or shared** with any third party for marketing or advertising purposes.

Data may be transmitted to the following services strictly as required to provide core functionality:

- **Anthropic (Claude AI):** Message text is sent to the Claude API for natural language processing and response generation.
- **Twilio:** SMS and MMS messages are routed through Twilio's communications platform for delivery.

No other third parties receive your data.

## Data Retention

- Data is retained on local private hardware for as long as it is useful to the service operator.
- The service operator may delete any or all data at any time.
- No copies are stored outside of privately owned hardware.

## Opt-Out

You may stop receiving messages at any time by replying **STOP** to any message from ARIA. You may also text **HELP** for assistance. See our [Terms and Conditions](terms-and-conditions) for full opt-out details.

## Children's Privacy

ARIA is a private, single-user system and is not directed at or available to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

## Contact

For questions or concerns about this privacy policy, contact the service operator at the ARIA phone number or through the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/expectbugs/aria).
