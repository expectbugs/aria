## Program Name

ARIA (Ambient Reasoning & Intelligence Assistant)

## Program Description

ARIA is a personal AI assistant that communicates via SMS and MMS messaging. By texting ARIA's phone number, you can ask questions, manage calendar events and reminders, receive daily briefings, share images and files for analysis, and interact with various personal assistant features. ARIA processes your messages using AI and responds via text message.

## Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your usage. ARIA responds to incoming messages and may send occasional proactive notifications such as reminders or alerts. You will not receive unsolicited marketing messages. Typical usage results in fewer than 50 messages per day.

## Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply. Standard messaging rates from your wireless carrier apply to all SMS and MMS messages sent to and received from ARIA. ARIA does not charge any additional fees for its messaging service. Contact your wireless carrier for details about your messaging plan.

## Support and Contact

For help or support, text **HELP** to the ARIA phone number at any time. You will receive a response with contact information and basic instructions. You may also reach the service operator through the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/expectbugs/aria).

## Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out of ARIA messaging at any time:

- Text **STOP** to the ARIA phone number to stop receiving all messages. You will receive a single confirmation message acknowledging your opt-out request. No further messages will be sent unless you re-subscribe.
- Text **HELP** to receive support information and instructions.

After opting out, you may resume messaging at any time by texting any message to the ARIA phone number.
